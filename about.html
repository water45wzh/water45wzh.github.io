About me

I am a senior undergraduate student of Peking University (PKU), majoring in Mathematics (Data Science) advised by Prof. Zhanxing Zhu on Deep Learning Theory, and double majoring in Psychology advised by Prof. Yan Bao on temporal perception. I also enjoy working with Prof. Xiaoou Chen on Sound and Music Computing/Digital Audio Tech. I’m currently visiting Prof. Gus Xia at NYU Shanghai on automatic composition.

In the industry, I interned at ByteDance AI Lab working on Automatic Music Generation and participating Audio Watermarking and Cover Song Identification, and previously interned at Microsoft Speech Group working on High-fidelity Singing Voice Synthesis. And I’m also very interested in Music Composition & Production, both traditional and electronic, so fortunate to have a wonderful experience with Prof. Fei Wang from Chinese Conservatory of Music since 2017, I also thank Composer Ye Liu for working with me on screen scoring in the real film industry. About music education, I helped Prof. Heinrich Taube (UIUC) to deal with the Chinese localization of his amazing automatic music theory education software, Harmonia.

My previous interests are mainly about math (especially discrete math) and musical composition (screen scoring, musical theater and others), and have tried a lot in my broad interests. Currently, my interest lies in the intersection of music and science & technology, aiming to chang the way people appreciate, create, perform, and teach music. With the help of my interdisciplinary background, like solid mathematics & psychology background, good coding ability and strong musicianship, I started by designing and analyzing MIR-related and automatic composition algorithms. During this process, I gradually realized that the goal of music-related technology is to serve humans to better interact with machines and other people on music experience. In order to better develop human-centered technology, I started my journey of psychology and cognitive science, began to research human perception and cognition. My future goal is to build next-generation music creation tools (like DAW) to help everyone more easily create their own music.

I'm now seeking for PhD or research-track master position in 2021 Fall. If you’re interested, please do not hesitate to contact me!
